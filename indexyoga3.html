YOGA

The word Yoga comes from Sanskrit word "Yuj" that means bonding of connection , join or unite. This implies joining or integrating all aspects of the individual-Body,Mind,and Spirit, to achieve a happy balanced and useful life and spiritually uniting the individual with the "Supreme", Yoga is considered as on of the six branches of classical philosophy and is refered through the 'Vedas' , ancient Indian scriptures, the oldest texts in existence.Yoga is a spiritual quest, Its purpose is to help each one of us achieve our highest potential and to experience enduring health and happiness.

HISTORY OF YOGA

Yoga is truly a science of human personality.The history of yoga is very old .

The available evidences show that history of yoga is related to the Indus Valley Civilisation. At that time people used to do yoga , in Hindi it is said "Yog".On basis of Various sculptures and scriptures , we reach the conclusion that yoga was a part of this civilisation.Yoga is also mentioned in "Mahabharata", "Ramayana"and Upanishads.Even Kabir, Tulsidas and Surdas had mentioned yoga in their writings, Yoga is main part of Indian culture.

BASICS OF YOGA

How to do yoga . For doing yoga you need a proper ventilated room or any outdoor peaceful place and a simple rubbermat.

Yogic practices should be performed in the morning after natural call. These practices should always be practised with empty stomach. The place for doing yogic practices should be clean and peaceful. The time for yogic exercises should be fixed. You should wear comfotable clothes during this tome. It should be done without any jerk and sudden force. Breathing should be taken through nose. The indivdual who is doing yoga regularly should take light diet. While doing yogic exercises, Concentration is essential for getting maximum results.
